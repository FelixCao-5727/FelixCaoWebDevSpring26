Easy Veggie Pasta

Ingredients

200g pasta (penne or fusilli work well)

1 jar (400g) marinara sauce

1 zucchini, diced

1 bell pepper, sliced

2 cloves garlic, minced

2 tbsp olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.

2. Saut√© garlic and vegetables in olive oil for 5-7 minutes.

3. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer.

4. Toss pasta with sauce and serve.
