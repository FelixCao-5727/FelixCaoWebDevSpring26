# Easy Veggie Pasta - Perfect for Breaks

![image of cooked pasta](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1563379926898-05f4575a45d8?ixlib=rb-1.2.1&auto=format&fit=crop&w=800&q=80)

## Ingredients

- 200g pasta (penne or fusilli work well)
- 1 jar (400g) marinara sauce
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

## Steps

1. Boil pasta according to package instructions until al dente
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
3. Saut√© garlic for 1 minute until fragrant
4. Add vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender
5. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 3 minutes
6. Drain pasta and add to the sauce mixture
7. Toss everything together until well combined
8. Season with salt, pepper, and top with Parmesan if desired
